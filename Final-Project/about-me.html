About Me

Hello to everyone viewing this page, My name is Roberto Carlos Colmenares Hernandez and I am currently enrolled at wake tech for a degree in Web Development. I am 21 years old and I was born and raised in Raleigh North Carolina. I enjoy web design because it combines the fun you get when creating something that you feel is enjoyable with the trial and error method of coding. A few languages that I am proficient at are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
